## What it does

/writing-style rewrites or generates content in your specific voice, based on a style guide you define. It enforces sentence length, vocabulary level, tone (direct vs. warm), structure preferences, and things you explicitly never want to say.

The output sounds like you, not like an AI that's trying to sound like you.

## When to use it

- Before publishing any external content (Substack, LinkedIn, documentation)
- When drafting communications that go out under your name
- When you want to sanity-check that something doesn't have AI slop patterns in it

## How to trigger it

Say: `apply my writing style to this` and paste the content

Or set it up once: `define my writing style` and answer the questions it asks

## What you need

A writing style definition (the skill will help you create one if you don't have it). Once defined, it lives in your memory files and applies automatically whenever you invoke the skill. Works without integrations.
